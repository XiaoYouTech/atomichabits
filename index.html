FAQ

Q: Does data sync across devices?

A: Yes! Real-time syncing between iPhone, Watch, and Widgets is powered by App Groups, keeping all your data perfectly consistent.

Q: Does it require an internet connection?

A: No. AtomicHabits runs completely locally to protect your privacy. An iCloud sync option will be provided in future updates.

Q: Can I customize my habits?

A: Absolutely! You can create any number of habits, set custom implementation intentions, and schedule smart reminders.

Q: What is the Identity System?

A: Based on the Atomic Habits philosophy, it lets you level up your identity through continuous check-ins, evolving from "Novice" to "Master" as the habit becomes part of who you are.
